Commission Art

A photo fades, but a painting stays. A pet's expression, a wedding day, a home someone loves, I can help you make those moments last.

Commission Process

i.

Send your photos

Use the commission form below, or email me directly, with your reference photos and a little context — who or what the piece is for, and the size you're picturing.

ii.

Secure your booking

I'll confirm receipt by email and send an invoice for a 50% deposit, which reserves your place in the queue.

iii.

Sketch & progress

I'll send progress photographs as the piece develops, starting with the composition sketch, so you can follow along and flag anything early.

iv.

Final approval

Once you're completely happy with the finished piece, I'll send an invoice for the remaining balance.

v.

Posted to you

Once payment is confirmed, I post the piece flat, tracked and insured, to the address of your choice.
